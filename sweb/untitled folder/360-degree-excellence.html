Social Service

Social service is integral to life at Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar. We instill in our students a sense of duty towards society, and sensitize them to the plight of the less fortunate in the world.

As part of our endeavor to shape our students into good human beings, we accompany them on frequent visits to orphanages, old age homes, hospitals and other places where they can spend quality time with the lesser privileged. Our students have a rather high level of engagement with all our social service initiatives, including fundraisers, donation drives and health camps.
