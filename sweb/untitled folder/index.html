    About Us

    Education is for life, not merely for living.” – Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

    Founded in 1976 by the grace of Sri Sathya Sai Baba, Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar is a co-education school which imparts education with an emphasis on character-building. We aim to shape children into motivated young men and women who contribute to the world around them by way of their intellect, hard work, honesty and love for humanity.
